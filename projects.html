Projects

  1. Snakes Game

    • Duration: 2 Weeks

    • Technologies Used:Python

    • Major or Minor: Minor

    • Brief Description:

      • The basic overview of the game is that we have to go through a maze, bound at certain places, beyond which the DOT would not move. The DOT would move according to keyboard inputs. We can choose the difficulty level. According to the chosen difficulty level, we set different timers. The aim of the game is to move through the maze and collect score colour DOTS that would appear at random points as we go on to collect it. As we collect the score DOTS more, the score count will continue to increase. And we continue to do so, until the timer is finished.
